About Us

Mission

Our mission is to unite all the stakeholders in the pharmaceutical industry to foster cooperation, knowledge-sharing, and mutual support. We are committed to promoting ethical practices, ensuring access to safe and quality medications, and elevating the standards of pharmacy practice within the district.

Advocate for the Interests of Members

We tirelessly work to protect the rights and interests of our members, ensuring their voices are heard in matters related to the pharmaceutical profession, business regulations, and policy-making.Our mission is to promote ethical practices and elevate pharmacy standards.

Continuing Education and Training

We organize workshops, seminars, and training sessions to keep our members updated with the latest advancements in the pharmaceutical field. By fostering continuous learning, we empower our members to deliver superior services to the community.

Community Outreach

As responsible stakeholders, we actively participate in community health programs, awareness campaigns, and initiatives that promote healthcare and wellness in the Nanded District.

Strengthening Industry Relations

We forge strong ties with pharmaceutical manufacturers, distributors, and regulatory bodies to promote a cohesive and dynamic pharmaceutical ecosystem.

Code of Ethics

Our association strictly adheres to a robust code of ethics, ensuring that our members maintain the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and compassion in their practices.
